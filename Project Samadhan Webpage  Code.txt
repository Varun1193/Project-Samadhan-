<p class="throwback">
More than 300 villages, mainly in Punjab's Ropar, Jalandhar and Ferozepur districts, were lamentably affected in floods by the Satluj 
river, damaging crops in thousands of acres and coercing thousands of villagers to spend nights on rooftops. Hit by one of the worst floods 
in the district in the last three decenniums, IIT-Ropar in Rupnagar, Punjab faced a true test of its grit. IIT-Ropar, which is situated
 near the banks of the Sutlej was the worst hit by the floods. Albeit the waters of Sutlej did not reach the campus, it was flooded by the rivulets
 Bhudki and Sangraon, which flow on the south-western side of the IIT campus. The frequent relinquishment of water from Bhakra had even worsened the
 situation. The campus and the nearby areas were submerged by the evening of 18th August. The water kept on entering the area and the situation became even
 worse. The campus was evacuated by 19th morning. The villages around the campus were submerged. Thousands of villagers were left homeless. Everything they 
possessed was gone. Our institution stood with them and availed those people in all aspect. They were given place in our beas hostel and the pabulum and 
medical accommodation were provided to them. The entire IIT fraternity stood together and had tackled this situation very intrepidly and was able to 
instaurate at an unbelievable pace.

<a name="motivation">
<p class="motivation">MOTIVATION</p>


<p class="get3">
There is a famous saying which goes as</p>
<p class="saying"><b><u>"The Secret to Happiness Is Helping Others"</u></b></p>
<p class="motivation1">And it is indeed true. Those people had lost their livelihood, their domicile, their belongings everything. We were fortuitous 
enough to recover expeditiously from this tragedy anon but they were not. Without any support, their life would have turned out very miserable. If
 someone is capable enough to avail someone, then he should. That's what we did. The most immensely colossal avail they required were monetary assistance.  With a noble conception and a unique approach, we were able to raise funds and contribute to the rehabilitation of those people.</p>
<p><a href="#start">back to top</a></p>
<p><a name="what we did"><p class="did">WHAT WE DID?</p></a>
<p class="did1">To help those people, we need a source through which we can earn money. So, we decided to design a tshirt that we will sell to the IIT
 fraternity. This tshirt will be designed by the students themselves, the whole fabrication and availability will be taken care by the group itself. And whatever profit we will make from the sale, will handover the same to the needy.
</p>
